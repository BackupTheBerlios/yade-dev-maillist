Hi everybody,

I just subscribed the yade-dev team to yade's bugs, since bug tracking
has become an important means of communication (besides the yade-dev
list that was used for this purpose before). You will also receive
commit messages from the bzr repository (as you used to from svn).

Setup your mail filters accordingly so that you don't get bunch of
e-mails in your inbox but rather to a special mail folder; sorry for
inconvenience.

--- paste from the subscription page: ---

Managing Launchpad Bugs e-mail

Every e-mail message sent by the Launchpad Bug Tracker includes one or
more X-Launchpad-Bug: headers. You can use tools such as procmail to
create custom filters based on this header.

--- end paste ---

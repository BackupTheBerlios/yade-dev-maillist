Where scene_test.xml is a 4000 particles triaxial test, with enabled 
autoCompressionActivation, a low strain rate  (strainRate="0.01"), and a

else is default IIRC.

real    13m10.555s
user    12m33.367s
sys     0m4.988s

you do something else during computation.
